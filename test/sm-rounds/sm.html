How to Play

  1. March 10 – March 16 draft the top colors you want to see in this year's Swatch Madness tournament.
  2. Starting March 17, complete your bracket one round at a time. There are 4 rounds through April 5 and we want to know your favorites – don't miss your chance to vote each round!
  1. Enter for a chance to win Sherwin-Williams prizes after completing each round. See Official Sweepstakes Rules for details.
  2. Use #SwatchMadness to share the fun with your friends!
